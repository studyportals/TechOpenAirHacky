About DT50

Google, McKinsey and Rocket Internet have joined forces to create the Digital Top 50 Awards to reward and celebrate the bold and trailblazing work of tomorrow’s top entrepreneurs.
The Digital Top 50 Awards recognise the top entrepreneurial talent in Europe, based on the following five categories: B2B Start-ups, B2C Start-ups, B2B Scale-ups, B2C Scale-ups, and Tech for Social Impact Companies. Participants will have their companies and ideas reviewed by a jury of key figures in the European tech, consulting, academic, corporate, and investment communities.

How does it work

Vote your favourite Startup online before 18 June
Come to TOA to see the Live Pitches on 21 June
Winners Criteria: 60% Jury, 20% Public, 20% Live Pitches
Enjoy our Ceremony and Drinks at TOA on 21 June

Syrona
Women’s health has lacked innovation due to historic systematic underinvestment. Syrona brings innovation to the femtech industry. Syrona does this by offering low cost , non-invasive digitised home-tests to empower women to monitor their own gynaecological health at home. The three main issues Syrona addresses currently is early detection of Fertility state, Endometriosis and Gynaecological cancers.
Bomberbot

Bomberbot is on the mission to empower girls and boys all around the world with digital skills to make them successful citizens in the 21st century. The Bomberbot platform helps teachers in primary schools to teach computer programming and Digital Education in a simple way. Bomberbot has been used by more than 130.000 children in Finland and the Netherlands

EVA

Cities are a hostile and challenging environment for the blind. 30% of them never leave their homes without human assistance. EVA is a wearable assistive tool. This pair of smart-glasses can give verbal guidance to it’s blind wearers. It uses sensor fusion and machine vision to give precise position and orientation data both indoor and outdoor and it is able to detect specific obstacles as well, empowering the blind to ambulate independently.

Isahit

Isahit is an impact sourcing platform for digital projects. Isahit provides services around management of data, pre-machine learning data set and any kind of moderation on social networks. This service is performed by young women in Africa today and comes as an additional income for those women so they can achieve their project of life (finance their studies, start an entrepreneurial project, or simply improve their living conditions).

Kiron Open Higher Education

Kiron Open Higher Education is a nonprofit EdTech organization using digital innovation and strategic collaborations with universities and online education providers to bridge the gap between refugees and higher education. Kiron’s offer consists of tailor-made curricula, a digital collaborative platform and a range of supportive services that enable refugees to start studying online while they work towards fulfilling university requirements.

Pedius

Pedius is a communication system helping Deaf and Hard of Hearing people to make phone calls, using voice recognition and speech synthesis technologies. Since 2013 Pedius helped more than 20.000 users in 10 countries to make their first phone call

AID Tech

AID:Tech’s data logistics solutions leverage Blockchain technology and digital identity to enable the delivery of digital entitlements including welfare, remittances, healthcare, aid and peer-to-peer donations. Our solutions equip our clients and partners including governments and donors, to better target users by leveraging data. We address fraud, corruption and inefficiencies in service delivery.

Madaster

Madaster is the global online register for real estate data on materials and products. It is our mission to facilitate a circular economy where waste can be eliminated by providing materials with an identity. Through data services for building owners, constructors, financiers and designers, it brings transparency in the circular and financial value of buildings. Madaster carefully focuses on governance, privacy, security and continuity.

Betterplace.org

betterplace.org is Germany’s largest charitable donation platform. Since its foundation in 2007 nearly 60 million € have been donated to more than 25,000 social projects worldwide. The platform is financed by offering digital solutions to companies for use in their CSR strategies, as well as through private donations and support from strategic partners. betterplace is run by the gut.org non-profit company, a social enterprise based in Berlin.

Deedmob

Deedmob is the online volunteering platform connecting volunteers, charities, companies, and government institutions to increase collaboration and maximize social impact. Like your regular volunteering website, Deedmob matches volunteers with nonprofits. But it doesn’t stop there. Our CRM tools and analytics help social organisations to create better volunteering experiences, so that volunteers want to come back to do more good.
